Anatomed 3D: AR Application

Capstone Project 2023: An Augmented Reality a supplementary Material to showcase pre-medical student.

Augmented reality has distinct advantages over traditional learning environments and can have an impact on the learning process. The purpose of this studywas to assess the effectiveness of the developed Augmented Reality as a supplement for learning Human Anatomy for pre-medical students, based on the perceptions of medical teachers and pre-medical students.

The study would be able to provide a potential solution and starting point for developing better Augmented Reality software that aids in human anatomy education. The study conducted a survey based on ISO 25010, a software and quality checker, regarding the effectiveness of the developed software asan Augmented Reality (AR) application in terms of Performance Efficiency, Functional Suitability, Reliability, and Usability.

Enhance

Software Engineering Project 2022: English Enhancement App for Kids

In our Software Engineering Course, we have been assigned the task of creating a mini-thesis on an English Enhancement Application. This topic holds significant importance as it addresses the growing concern of declining reading comprehension skills among children worldwide. To tackle this issue, our application aims to incorporate engaging graphical elements and interactive features that can help improve reading comprehension abilities in young learners. By blending educational content with visually appealing graphics, we aim to create a user-friendly platform that motivates and encourages children to develop stronger reading skills. Our goal is to make learning a fun and enjoyable experience, nurturing a generation of confident and proficient readers.
